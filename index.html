Centre for Innovation & Research (CIR) seeks to be the leading institution for in-depth innovation, research, and development to promote inclusive policymaking in Bangladesh, and strengthen regional and global economic integration.


The Centre for Innovation and Research (CIR) is committed to fostering a culture of discovery, creativity, and innovation that drives progress and transforms lives. Our Research and Innovation Hub serves as the nucleus of excellent research and visionary thinking. With a rich tapestry of expertise across various disciplines, and a relentless pursuit of knowledge, we are shaping the future through our research endeavors.

Our Vision

To be a pioneering hub that fosters groundbreaking innovations and cutting-edge research, empowering individuals and industries to solve global challenges, drive sustainable growth, and inspire transformative change through collaboration, creativity, and technology.

Our Mission

To create an ecosystem that nurtures curiosity, drives impactful research, and accelerates innovation across various fields. By collaborating with academia, industry, and communities, we aim to generate transformative solutions that address current and future challenges, enhance knowledge, and contribute to the sustainable development of society and the economy. Serving the growing demand of nation of Bangladesh for a demand-driven and accountable development process by stimulating information, generating knowledge and influencing policymaking through innovation and research.

Our Objectives

  1. Foster Innovation: Encourage the development of creative ideas and solutions that address real-world challenges across various industries and sectors.
  2. Promote Collaborative Research: Facilitate partnerships between academia, industry, and government to enhance the quality and applicability of research
  3. Advance Knowledge: Drive the pursuit of new knowledge through cutting-edge research and experiments, contributing to the global body of scientific and technological understanding.
  4. Support Sustainable Development: Focus on research and innovation that promotes environmental sustainability, economic growth, and social well-being.
  5. Develop Practical Solutions: Translate research findings into practical applications that benefit society, businesses, and communities.
  6. Encourage Entrepreneurial Spirit: Nurture an entrepreneurial mindset among researchers, offering support to transform innovative ideas into startups and scalable businesses.
  7. Provide Knowledge Transfer: Share research outcomes and innovations with relevant stakeholders, ensuring their implementation in industry, policy-making, and public services.
  8. Cultivate Talent: Provide training and mentoring to students, researchers, and professionals, building a pipeline of skilled innovators and researchers.
  9. Promote Interdisciplinary Research: Encourage collaboration across various disciplines to address complex, multifaceted problems with innovative solutions.
  10. Create a Global Impact: Position the center as a leader in innovation and research, contributing to the advancement of science, technology, and society on a global scale.
